The method · No. 13
How to calculate
your Barista FI,
in four steps.
01
Calculate monthly expenses
Every dollar you spend monthly. e.g. $3,000/mo.
→ This sets both your Barista FI and Full FIRE targets.
02
Subtract planned part-time income
Barista, freelance, consulting — any Phase 2 income.
→ e.g. $3,000 − $1,200 part-time = $1,800 gap.
03
Multiply gap × 300
$1,800 × 300 = $540,000 Barista FI target.
→ The number that ends full-time work. The key formula.
04
Track portfolio to target
Once you hit it, Phase 2 begins. Work part-time.
→ Compound growth then closes the gap to Full FIRE.
$3k − $1.2k = $1.8k × 300 = $540,000 Barista FI target
