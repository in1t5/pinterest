Myth I
Busted
"Barista FIRE means literally working as a barista."
TRUTH
Any part-time income counts — freelance, consulting, part-time roles. The name is a metaphor.
Myth II
Busted
"You need to save 25× expenses first."
TRUTH
Barista FI target is 12–15× expenses — roughly half of Full FIRE. You can exit full-time work much sooner.
Myth III
Busted
"Part-time work kills portfolio growth."
TRUTH
Phase 2 is when compound growth does its heaviest lifting. Reduced withdrawals accelerate growth.
