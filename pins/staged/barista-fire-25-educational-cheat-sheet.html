Barista FIRE Calculator

5 Numbers to Know
before you calculate.

01
Monthly Expenses
Your baseline figure. Every dollar you spend per month — start here, everything else derives from this number.
e.g. $3,000/mo
02
Part-time Income Target
What you plan to earn in Phase 2 — barista, freelance, consulting, or any part-time work you'd actually enjoy.
e.g. $1,200/mo
03
Monthly Income Gap
Expenses minus part-time income. This gap is the only thing your portfolio needs to cover.
e.g. $3k − $1.2k = $1,800/mo
04
Barista FI Portfolio
Monthly gap × 300. The portfolio where part-time income plus returns cover all expenses.
e.g. $1,800 × 300 = $540,000
05
Full FIRE Portfolio
Annual expenses × 25 (the 4% rule). Your final destination — no income needed, ever.
e.g. $36k × 25 = $900,000
