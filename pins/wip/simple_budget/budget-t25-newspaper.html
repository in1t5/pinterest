Household of four
saves €700 a month

By the numbers · with our free budget calculator
They didn't earn more. They tracked spending for thirty days, found three subscriptions they'd forgotten about, and moved €700 to savings on the first of every month before spending anything.
The 50/30/20 rule handled the rest. Half for needs, three-tenths for wants, two-tenths for the future. No categories. No spreadsheet paranoia. Just a monthly budget planner.
The calculator takes four inputs — income, fixed costs, variable spend, savings goal — and returns a plan in sixty seconds. You'll recognise the feeling: relief.
