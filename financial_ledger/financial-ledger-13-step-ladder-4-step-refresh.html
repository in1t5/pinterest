The method · No. 13
How to calculate
your net worth,
in four steps.
01
List all your assets
Savings, investments, property, pension.
→ Everything you own that has value.
02
List all your liabilities
Mortgage, loans, credit cards, overdrafts.
→ Everything you owe to someone else.
03
Subtract to get net worth
Assets − liabilities = the number that matters.
→ Positive and growing? You're on track.
04
Log it in Financial Ledger
Repeat once a year. Watch your wealth grow.
→ Free, browser-based, no sign-up needed.
